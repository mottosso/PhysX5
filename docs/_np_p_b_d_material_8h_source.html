41 // Compared to other objects, materials are special since they belong to the SDK and not to scenes
42 // (similar to meshes). That's why the NpPBDMaterial does have direct access to the core material instead
43 // of having a buffered interface for it. Scenes will have copies of the SDK material table and there
44 // the materials will be buffered.
