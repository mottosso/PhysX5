PxU32 physx::Gu::AdjTriangle::mATri[3]

Links/References of adjacent triangles. The 2 most significant bits contains the counterpart edge in the adjacent triangle. mATri[0] refers to edge 0-1 mATri[1] refers to edge 0-2 mATri[2] refers to edge 1-2
