Detailed Description

A structure responsible for: storing an aabb representation for each active shape in the related scene managing the creation/removal of aabb representations when their related shapes are created/removed updating all aabbs that require an update due to modification of shape geometry or transform updating the aabb of all aggregates from the union of the aabbs of all shapes that make up each aggregate computing and reporting the incremental changes to the set of overlapping aabb pairs.
