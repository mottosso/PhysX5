Detailed Description

Temporary CCD representation for a shape.

Stores data about a shape that may be frequently used in CCD. It also stores update counters per-shape that can be compared with the body's update counter to determine if the shape needs its transforms re-calculated. This avoids us needing to store a list of shapes in a CCD body.
